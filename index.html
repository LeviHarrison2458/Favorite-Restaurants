My Favorite Places to Eat

Hello! Here is a list of my five favorite pizza restaurants to eat in Chicago!

  1. Uno's Pizza
  2. Palermo's
  3. Beggar's
  4. Aurelio's
  5. Lou Malnati's
  6. My Kitchen
Uno's Palermo's Beggars Aurelio's Lou Malanti's
1 2 3 4 5

Uno's

At number one my favorite pizza restaurant is Uno's. Uno's pizza is located at 29 E Ohio St. They are known for their delicious deep dish pizza.

I usually order a large four-cheese deep dish with spinach.

Palermo's

At number two Palermo's is my second favorite pizza restaurant in Chicago. It is located at 16644 S Oak Park, Tinley Park, IL.

A cheese deep dish from Palermo's is easily number two on this list.

Beggars

Numer three was difficult, but I decided to give Beggars pizza this spot. It is located at 310 S Clinton St

The crust on this pizza is not as thick as a deep dish, but that's ok because the taste is magnificent.

Aurelio's

For number four I decided to go with Aurelios. Aurelios is located on 1212 S Michigan Ave

This is another thin crust pizza with cheese and sausage!

Lou Malnati's

Lastly, Lou Malnati's made my top five list of favorite pizza places in Chicago. It is located at 410 North Michigan Avenue

This pizza has a lot of tomato sauce and crunchy crust.
